A Little Bit About Me

After promoting pharmaceuticals for 20 years, I decided I needed a change. I discovered SkillCrush online and started taking classes. I found that I love writing clean, elegant code and creating beautiful and functional websites. I did some research and found the biggest complaints about web developers are lack of communication and sub-par service. That is why I have built my business with that research in mind.

See, I think your business is a big deal. That's why I provide excellent communication throughout the project and my goal is to deliver top notch service every time to every client regardless of the size of the business. I ask lots of questions and have a needs-based approach to your web design.
